How It Works

Three steps from signup to classroom impact.

01

Volunteer

High schoolers sign up through their state chapter. No prerequisites — just enthusiasm for civic education and a willingness to learn.

02

Train

Complete YCN's structured training program. Learn our bipartisan curriculum, classroom management basics, and age-appropriate teaching methods.

03

Teach

Lead engaging civics workshops for elementary and middle school students. Real impact, real classrooms, real change in your community.

Democracy starts in the classroom.
